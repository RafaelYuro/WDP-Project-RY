Rockhopper and Rosalina

Who are they?

Rockhopper Hudson and Rosalina Karam are hosts and guides of this game. Their goal is to help with teaching PEMDAS and making sure you have fun too. Rockhopper knows his way around PEMDAS and loves having fun with his friends. But when he needs to, he'll keep his friends in check. He loves, adventure, cookies, volley ball, and is protective. Rosalina knows how organize and set up plans. But knows how to set up something fun. She loves partying, cotton candy, cheerleading, and is organized.
